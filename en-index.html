Welcome to my website

Timothy Veryser is a young Belgian tenor who performs on international stages. In 2024 he graduated summa cum laude in Classical Singing from the School of Arts Ghent. He has sung at the Bregenz Festival, La Monnaie Opera House, Opera Ballet Vlaanderen, and the Beethoven Festival in Zutphen. He has won several prizes in international singing competitions, including the first prize and audience award at the XIV Concours Lyrique International Bell'Arte and the third prize at the National Operetta Competition.
